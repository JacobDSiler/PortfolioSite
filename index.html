About

Builder by nature.
Storyteller by calling.

I'm an independent developer, fiction author, and homeschooling father of five daughters, based in Mullingar, County Westmeath, Ireland. I operate under Siler Technology — a one-person studio that ships tools and projects across multiple disciplines.

My work spans Blender addons, Unreal Engine 5 plugins, single-file web apps, hardware prototypes, and the Sky Bridge Saga — a multi-book steampunk fantasy series co-authored with my sister Allyson Siler.

I build in cycles. Each project gets full attention until it's done, then I move to the next problem worth solving. Everything is made to be useful, playable, or both.

The Sky Bridge
Saga

A steampunk fantasy series set on Stella — a world modeled on the Pleiades star cluster. Seven regions on the continent of Pleione, an ancient Blackthorn family legacy, and a story that spans multiple books and two authors.
